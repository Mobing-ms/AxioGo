## 📖 About

AxioGo is an enterprise-grade **Data Intelligence Platform** that unifies modern data engineering, business intelligence, and agentic AI into a single ecosystem.

Built on a Lakehouse architecture, AxioGo transforms raw enterprise data into trusted, analytics-ready insights and enables **AXIS** to reason over enterprise knowledge, generate intelligent reports, automate workflows, and support data-driven decision making.

Unlike traditional AI assistants, AXIS is designed as an **Enterprise Intelligence Copilot** that understands business context, analyzes data, and delivers actionable insights—not just conversations.
